Frequently Asked Questions

What is the refund policy?

Our refund policy allows for a full refund within 14 days of purchase, provided that you have not accessed more than 20% of the course material or attempted any certification exams.

How do I reset my password?

You can reset your password by clicking the "Login" button, then selecting the "Forgot Password" link. An email with instructions will be sent to your registered address.

Are CertiCore certifications recognized by employers?

Yes, all CertiCore certifications are vendor-neutral and globally recognized. They are designed in collaboration with industry experts to validate the most in-demand tech skills.

How long do I have access to course materials?

Upon purchase, you receive lifetime access to all video lectures and downloadable resources for the course you enrolled in. You can learn at your own pace and revisit the material anytime.

Still need help?

If you can't find the answer you're looking for, please don't hesitate to reach out to our support team.
